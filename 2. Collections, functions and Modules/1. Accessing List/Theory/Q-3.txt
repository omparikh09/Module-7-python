Q-3. Slicing a list: accessing a range of elements.

ans:
    1. Basic Slicing :-
        Extract a subset using start and end indices.

    2. Using Steps :-
        The step parameter determines the jump between elements.

        -> A positive step moves forward.
        -> A negative step reverses the order.

    3. Slicing with Negative Indexing :-
        Negative indexing allows slicing from the end.
